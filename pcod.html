PCOD / PCOS

Understand what it means, symptoms to notice, and gentle management.

What is PCOD / PCOS?

Polycystic Ovarian Disease (PCOD) or Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS) is a hormonal condition commonly affecting women of reproductive age. It impacts the ovaries' ability to function properly and may cause irregular periods, cysts, increased androgens, acne, and changes in weight.

Common Symptoms

  • Irregular or missed menstrual cycles
  • Weight gain or difficulty losing weight
  • Acne, oily skin, or hair thinning
  • Excess facial/body hair growth
  • Difficulty with ovulation

Gentle Ways to Manage PCOS

  • Maintain a balanced nutrient-rich diet
  • Stay active and exercise regularly
  • Manage stress through self-care habits
  • Monitor your cycle for better insights
  • Consult a doctor for guided treatment
