🌸 VELORA – AI-Powered Menstrual Health Companion

🚀 Overview

VELORA is a cloud-based menstrual health tracking and AI-powered assistant designed 
to provide accurate cycle predictions, health awareness, and personalized insights for women.
Unlike traditional period trackers, VELORA goes beyond basic date prediction by integrating AI-based analysis, 
educational support, nutritional guidance, and a secure community platform.

❗ Problem Statement
Many existing menstrual tracking solutions:
-Focus only on cycle prediction
-Lack awareness about PCOD/PCOS and health conditions
-Raise privacy concerns due to third-party integrations
-Do not provide personalized insights or explanations

VELORA addresses these gaps by providing a secure, intelligent, and comprehensive health platform.

💡 Key Features

🩸 Cycle Tracking & Prediction
-Log menstrual cycle dates and symptoms.AI-based prediction using historical data,Provides explanations for predictions

🤖 AI Chatbot Assistant
-Answers user queries.Provides self-assessment and guidance

🧠 Health Awareness
-Information about PCOD/PCOS, fertility, hygiene.Educational content for better understanding

🥗 Nutritional Guidance
-Suggests healthy food alternatives.Links symptoms with possible nutrient needs

💬 Community Chatroom
-Secure platform for sharing experiences.Encourages open discussion and support

🔐 Privacy & Security
-No third-party data sharing.Secure authentication and data storage

☁️ Cloud-Based System
-Multi-device access.No installation required
